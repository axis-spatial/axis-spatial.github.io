About Us

Our Vision

To be the leading company in Africa offering GIS services.

Our Mission

To spearhead GIS innovation and growth in Africa.

Our Profile

We are a startup based in Nairobi, Kenya offering geospatial information systems services such as internet and web mapping, GIS system development, geofencing and business intelligence. We are proprietors of Zunguka Navigational System used in several campuses in Kenya.


We embrace open technology, agile development and user-centered design to build relevant impactful and sustainable products for our clients.

How We Work

1. Identify

We begin by identifying a problem that can be solved using geospatial information systems (GIS) services. This includes a thorough system analysis to conceptualize the processes involved in a system and how they can be improved using GIS technology.

2. Develop

Next up, we engage your team and ours to develop prototypes of possible solution(s) to this problem. We do this using agile-development methodologies as well as continually engaging the you in the process.

3. Implement

We then implement a chosen prototype and deploy a fully testested and functional GIS solution with possible improvements and support.
